'Better Off' Illustration

2013 / Illustration / Talenthouse Competition

Illustration for HAIM’s single “Better Off” as a part of the Talenthouse and Secret 7" competition. This work was one of the chosen ones to be printed as a vinyl cover and was featured on the Secret 7" exhibition at the Mother London gallery.

Words in our mind are like people in our lives, they come and go. The words in this illustration, symbolise the people in movement. As our thoughts, they are always changing, just like humans.

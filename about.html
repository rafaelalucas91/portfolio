27 year old graphic designer based in Oeiras, currently working as a UI Designer at Fullsix, one of the biggest creative agencies in Portugal. Always having great curiosity regarding the artistic world, Rafaela always knew she would be somewhere in between.

With a degree in Multimedia Art at the Faculty of Fine Arts of Lisbon, she then did a one year course in Graphic Design at Restart Instituto de Artes e Criatividade, before jumping into her Master's Degree in Graphic Design and Editorial Projects at the Faculty of Fine Arts, in Porto.
She started working part-time while studying, at a Photography Studio doing post-production and photo retouching, skills that were most valuable when she started her internship for her Master's Degree, in Colönia Design Studio, in Porto. Having the opportunity to work as a graphic designer and photography assistant while studing in another city was a great way for her to grow as an artist as well as a person.

After finishing her studies, Rafaela worked for a year and a half in a wine company, Darwin Bev, as a graphic designer and creative strategist, acquiring good skills in label design and packaging. Nowadays, she still works as a freelancer for Quevedo Port Wine.
Having a background mostly in the offline world, she is now having the opportunity to be a part of the UI Design team at Fullsix, another area that always had her interest. If you want to know a bit more about her or have any questions, feel free to send an e-mail to hello@rafaelalucas.com.

Uma Avenida, Três Vidas

2014 / Editorial Design / Master's Degree Work

A book about three cafés situated in Avenida dos Aliados (Porto, Portugal) in the decade of 1930, Monumental, Guarany and Imperial. It includes the story of the building as well as what it is currently used for.

Group Work with: Diana Condeço, Rute de Carvalho and Sofia Veiga.
